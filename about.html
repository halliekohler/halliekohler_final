Hello!


We're Farrah & Hallie, pleased to meet you.

Farrah was born and raised in the Metro-Detroit area, and now lives in New York, NY.

She graduated with a BFA in Professional Photographic Illustration and a minor in Entrepreneurship and a concentration in Women's Gender Studies from Rochester Institute of Technology.

She currently works as a freelance digital tech, retoucher, and photo assistant.

Hallie also hails from the Mitten state, up north on the shore of Lake Michigan.

After graduating from Colgate University, she hopped aboard a renovated school bus to travel the country shooting a web documentary series. A year spent living in a remote north woods cabin kickstarted her freelance career, as well as a small greeting card company she owns and designs for.

She is currently living in Brooklyn, NY, freelancing photography, videography, illustration, and design, as well as learning how to code (and loving it).
